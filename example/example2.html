jQuery.confine Example 2

This example demonstrates

  1. implicitly confining a textarea via HTML5 data attributes on the textareas
  2. Support for confining dynamically added textareas after the plugin activation

Steps to test:

  1. Try typing in more than the maximum characters for each textarea
  2. Note that each has a separate maximum, defined by their data-maxlength HTML5 attributes
  3. Click 'Add Another Textarea' to dynamically add more fields, and note they are also confined by their attributes, even though they were created after the confine plugin was initialized.
